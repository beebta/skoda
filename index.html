⚠️ WARNING: Imran - Second Hand Car Dealer Scam Alert

This page serves as a warning to potential car buyers

BEWARE OF FRAUDULENT PRACTICES

This page documents multiple complaints and reports from customers who have had negative experiences with Imran, a second-hand car dealer.

Reported Scam Tactics:

1. Odometer Tampering

Multiple reports indicate that vehicles sold by Imran have had their odometers rolled back to show lower mileage than actual.

2. Undisclosed Accident History

Vehicles sold as "accident-free" were later found to have been in major accidents with significant repairs.

3. False Mechanical Condition

Many customers report serious mechanical issues appearing shortly after purchase, despite being assured the vehicles were in excellent condition.

4. Refusal to Honor Warranties

Alleged warranty claims are often denied with various excuses, leaving buyers with expensive repairs.

5. Pressure Sales Tactics

Customers report being pressured into quick decisions without proper inspection or test drives.

6. Selling Cars with Outstanding Loans

Multiple victims report that Imran sells vehicles that still have existing loans on them, creating legal and ownership complications for buyers.

7. False Repair Promises

Imran promises to fix any problems that arise after purchase but consistently fails to follow through on these commitments.

8. Disappearing After Payment

Once payment is received, Imran typically switches off his phone and blocks buyers, making it impossible to contact him for support or warranty claims.

9. Loan Fraud

Imran frequently asks customers for personal loans, promising to repay but never returning the money.

What You Should Do:

  • Always get an independent mechanical inspection before purchasing any used vehicle
  • Request a complete vehicle history report
  • Be wary of deals that seem too good to be true
  • Research the dealer thoroughly before making any commitments
  • Consider paying with secure payment methods that offer buyer protection
